üîí Privacy Policy

At HALAJOBS.QA, your privacy is our top priority. Here's how we handle your information:

  • No Unnecessary Data Collection: We don't collect personal information from visitors or job seekers unless necessary.
  • Employer Contact Info: Job posts may include contact details (email, WhatsApp) provided directly by employers.
  • No Data Selling: We never sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties.
  • Anonymous Likes: Job likes are stored locally in your browser for privacy.
  • Job Expiration: Job postings are kept fresh and relevant.
  • Responsible Use: By using this platform, you agree to use information responsibly and only for job-related purposes.
