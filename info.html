About Us

Halajobs.qa is a simple, user-friendly job search platform designed to connect job seekers with employers in Qatar. Our mission is to make job hunting easy, accessible, and transparent. Whether you are a fresh graduate, a skilled worker, or an experienced professional, Halajobs.qa provides you with the tools to explore opportunities in various industries.

We focus on simplicity — no complicated sign-ups, no barriers — just jobs at your fingertips. Employers can quickly post their vacancies, and job seekers can discover the latest opportunities instantly.

Privacy Policy

At Halajobs.qa, your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy outlines how we handle your information:

  • We do not collect unnecessary personal data from visitors or job seekers.
  • Job posts may include contact details (such as email or phone) provided directly by employers.
  • We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties.
  • Job postings automatically expire after 30 days to keep listings updated and relevant.
  • By using this platform, you agree to use the information responsibly and only for job-related purposes.

If you have questions regarding our privacy policy, please contact us at support@halajobs.qa.
